Control

Self-sewn straitjacket, rope, 2022

Performed with Josefine Anderlo

FABRIKKEN for Kunst og Design (Photos: Niklas Adrian Vindelev)

The physical and psychological architecture of control is explored through a personal confrontation rooted in the artist's experience with OCD. A hand-sewn straitjacket and rope form a tight composition in which the body is both captured and defined by its own structure. The performance navigates the tension between strength and vulnerability, dominance and submission.
